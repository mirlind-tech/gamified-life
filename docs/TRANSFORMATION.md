### Weekday Framework

```text

21:30-22:00 - Review and next-day setup
```

### Training Split (4 days)

- Day 1: Upper strength
- Day 2: Lower strength + core
- Day 3: Upper hypertrophy
- Day 4: Lower hypertrophy + loaded carries

- Progressive overload
- Protein minimum daily
- No skipped sleep two days in a row
